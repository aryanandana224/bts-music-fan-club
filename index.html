About BTS

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a globally popular South Korean music group. Their music spreads messages of self-love, hope, youth, and confidence. They have millions of fans worldwide known as ARMY. They are also known as the "21st Century Pop Icons" and combine high-energy performances with lyrics addressing mental health, self-love, and youth empowerment.
